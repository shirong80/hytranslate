Anatomy.

02 · Construction

Built on a 16×16 grid, the mark is two columns linked by a centred arrow. Nothing decorative — every stroke is doing work.

0,0 1024,0 0,1024 1024,1024

One gesture, two readings.

The crossbar is an arrow, not a line — pointing from the source pillar toward the destination pillar. Read fast, it's an H. Read again, it's translation in motion.

  • Canvas1024 × 1024 squircle, r228
  • Pillar76w × 432h, radius 8
  • Arrow stem288 × 60, centred y=512
  • Arrowhead84 wide, 140 tall taper
  • Optical centre+0 px (mathematical)
  • Tilt0° · zero rotation
